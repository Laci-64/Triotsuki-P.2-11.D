Neumann János

Feltaláló, Az infomatika egyik atyja és az egyik ha nem a leghíresebb magyar informatikus

In this project, we will present the careers and work of 3 well-known Hungarian IT professionals. All three are quite different from each other, but IT still connects them. preserved the memory of János Neumann and nurtured his legacy. Each person excels in the world of IT, but it is not certain that they will be remembered for their lasting inventions, but rather for their engaging personality or actions or their persistent determination for science.
